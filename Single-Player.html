Redux - Octo Valley

THE Splatoon 1 Single Player Port!

Did you ever want to play the Splatoon 1 Story Mode with Splatoon 3's new features, such as the Squid Surge and Squid Roll? Project Redux makes that possible. We're not just porting over the multiplayer content (Stages, Kits, Gears), but also the entire Single Player of Splatoon 1.

Project Redux is going to feature World 1-5 with ALL Levels, Collectables, Enviroment and more.
Currently, you can play the entire World 1 of Octo Valley. The Octo Valley Hub World is going to be released with Wave 3 (Image below)

Wave 3 - New Content

Besides adding new Multiplayer Maps, our Team is also working hard to add new Content for Octo Valley at each release.

Note: Not every Release automatically features new content for Octo Valley.

What's going to be added within Wave 3?

Wave 3 of Project Redux is going to add the Octo Valley Hub, World 2 + all of it's Levels and some additional content such as Idol reskins to fit with their Splatoon 1 versions.

How long will it take for you to finish the development of Octo Valley?

Our focus is currently set to finish the Multiplayer Content. You can imagine that each Map requires time, and Octo Valley maps aren't taking less considering that we have to adjust the Models, Lightnings and Map files. We're aiming to finish the entire mod by the end of 2024.
